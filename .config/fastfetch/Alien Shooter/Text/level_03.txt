MISSION 03: 
the disaster!

DESCRIPTION: 
to say that there is a disaster 
on base is to put things mildly.  
hordes of monsters roam the base 
and have killed all attending 
personnel. the monster armies 
are growing by the moment, and 
will soon threaten all humanity.
you must find the reasons behind 
this disaster, and destroy any 
and all monsters you encounter.

TIPS:    
patrols says indicate that there 
is no energy on this level, you 
have to find out the cause of 
this malfunction and restore the 
energy supply to the laboratories.
